About Me

Hi, I'm Dihan Randila!

I'm a passionate and creative web developer based in Galle, Sri Lanka. At the age of 17, I have already embarked on my journey to explore the world of programming and technology.

I recently completed a Python programming for beginners course at the University of Moratuwa, which laid the foundation for my programming skills. Since then, I have been captivated by the possibilities that coding offers and the power of building digital solutions.

As a web developer, I specialize in creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites. I enjoy the process of transforming ideas and designs into functional and interactive web experiences. With every project I undertake, my goal is to deliver high-quality solutions that meet both the technical and aesthetic aspects.

Apart from web development, I am also passionate about creating Telegram bots and sharing my knowledge through my YouTube channel. It's incredibly fulfilling to be able to inspire and teach others who are interested in this field.

In my free time, I love to stay updated with the latest trends in technology, explore new programming languages and frameworks, and engage in outdoor activities that help me rejuvenate and find inspiration.

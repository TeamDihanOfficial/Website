About Me

Hi, I'm Dihan Randila!

Welcome to my portfolio website! I'm a passionate programmer, web developer, and graphic designer based in Galle, Sri Lanka. From a young age, I have been fascinated by the world of technology and its potential to shape the future. This curiosity led me to pursue a Python programming for beginners course at the University of Moratuwa, where I honed my coding skills and discovered my love for programming.

I specialize in creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites that deliver exceptional user experiences. With a keen eye for design and a passion for clean code, I bring ideas to life by blending creativity and technical expertise. My goal is to create websites that not only look stunning but also provide seamless functionality and intuitive navigation.

Besides web development, I am deeply interested in Telegram bot development. I love exploring the possibilities of bots and creating innovative solutions to simplify tasks and enhance user experiences. You can find tutorials and insights on my YouTube channel, where I share my knowledge and experiences in bot development.

When I'm not coding, you can find me indulging in my other passions. As a music lover, I enjoy exploring different genres and playing musical instruments. I also find inspiration in nature and love capturing its beauty through photography. Connecting with like-minded individuals and collaborating on exciting projects is something that always excites me.
